About Angelene Ku

Multi faceted violinist and educator, Angelene Ku is a graduate of University of Michigan (Violin Performance, BM) and Boston University (Violin Performance, MM). Angelene is working on the prestigious Artist Diploma program at Texas Christian University, starting August, 2024. Angelene was a full tuition scholarship recipient at the University of Michigan. She has studied under Lucia Lin (Boston Symphony Orchestra) and David Halen (Concertmaster of the St. Louis Symphony)

Angelene has received masterclasses throughout her studies by prestigious musical figures such as, Ilya Kaler, John Largess, Kurt Sassmannshaus, Grigory Kalinovsky, Simon James, and many others.

She has appeared at music festivals such as Innsbrook Summer Music Academy, Napa Valley Music Festival, Texas Chamber Music Institute. During Napa Valley Music Festival, as an orchestra fellow, she shared the stand with David Kim (Concertmaster of Philadelphia Orchestra) and Katherine Fong (Assistant Principal 2nd of the MET Orchestra). Angelene was also a full scholarship recipient for the Texas Chamber Music Institute where she also worked as a Resident Assistance. Aside from music festivals, she also performed for Michael Buble’s national tour in Grand Rapids, MI.

Her passion in Jazz led her participate in Boston University’s Jazz Combo and University of Michigan’s Contemporary Arts Orchestra.

As an avid educator, Angelene has worked with various institutions and organizations as a violin instructor such as Sphinx Organization, City Strings, Michigan Artist Citizen in partnership with University of Michigan, to name a few.
